Weather Analysis:

Wind Speed

Wind Speed observations around the worlds cities present a few noticable trends:

During December over 95% of the data points have between 0-10 km/h speeds spread evenly amongst latitude points.

Only 4 outlier data points fall within 15-17 km/h.

Analysis of global wind speeds is valuable to the renewable wind power sector. These types of analyses help guide the best ROI for establishing new locations for wind farms.

"Global maps of average wind speed help researchers determine where to develop wind energy. Wind turbines (high-tech windmills) can generate power in places far from power plants and without an electricity grid— but planners need to know where there is sufficient wind for the turbines to operate efficiently."

Nasa: Global Wind Speed
