Temperature

The major observable trend in the teperature data can be seen analysis of Max Temperature results. In the Max Temperature vs. Latitude scatter plot The countries above and below the equator (Latitude of 0) begin to recede in temperature the further they are away from the equator in a consistant pattern. This data is consistant with the relationship between sunlight and the equatorial cities, these areas on average recieve are more steady supply and sunlight and therefore heat regardless of seasons comparitively to cities that reside further from the equator.

"The further a location is from the equator, the less sunlight that location receives to heat the atmosphere and thus, the temperature is colder. The reason that higher latitudes receive less sunlight is due to the shape of Earth."
